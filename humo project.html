About Us

In Uzbek cuisine, samsa is mainly prepared in a round shape, covered in a tandoor. Nowadays, it is also cooked in a triangular and rectangular shape in gas ovens. To prepare samsa, dough is kneaded from wheat flour and a skin is made. Samsa is often served with vinegar, tomato sauce, and vegetable oil for Jizzakh samsa.
